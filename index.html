BUGEMA UNIVERSITY

Bugema University is a private, co-educational Ugandan university affiliated with the Seventh-day Adventist Church.
It is a part of the Seventh-day Adventist education system, the world's second largest Christian school system.

Bugema University is composed of the following schools:

  • School of Computing & Informatics.
  • School of Business.
  • School of Social Sciences.
  • School of Education.
  • School of Natural Sciences.
  • School of Graduate Studies.

    • Bugema University is composed of the following Departments:

  • Department of Public Administration and Management.
  • Department of Social Work and Social Administration.
  • Department of System Engineering.
  • Department of Journalism and Mass Communication.
